üåç Project Overview

EcoScan is a full-stack web app that leverages the Gemini 2.5 Flash API to educate users on recyclable materials via an AI-powered scanning system. We gamify recycling to boost engagement, turning everyday waste into rewarding habits with points, leaderboards, and impact tracking. Built to promote sustainability, it helps users reduce landfill waste one scan at a time.

Photo Analysis

Gamified Tracker

Facility Locator

Discover nearby recycling centers with easy directions via Geoapify.

Personal History

Review past scans and visualize your environmental impact over time.
